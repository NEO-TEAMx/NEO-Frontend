Frequently Asked Questions

What is cloud mining?

Cloud mining is the process of using remote data centers to mine cryptocurrencies. Instead of purchasing and maintaining your own mining hardware, you can rent computing power from a cloud mining provider and receive a share of the rewards based on the amount of computing power you've contributed.

How does Anchor Cloud Mining work?

Anchor Cloud Mining offers a user-friendly platform that allows you to purchase mining hashrates and start earning rewards immediately. Our data centers are located in secure facilities with high-speed internet connections, and we use the latest mining hardware and software to optimize your mining experience.

What cryptocurrencies can I mine in Anchor Mining ?

We only offer hashrates for mining anchor protocol(ANC). Our hashrates are designed to be flexible and affordable, allowing you to choose the option that best suits your needs and budget.

How much can I earn with Anchor Cloud Mining?

Your earnings will depend on a variety of factors, including the hashrate you're using for mining, the amount of computing power you've contributed, and the current market conditions. We provide detailed information about expected earnings for each of our mining plans, so you can make an informed decision.

How long does it take to receive my earnings?

We pay out mining rewards on a daily basis, so you can expect to receive your earnings within 24 hours of making a withdrawal request. Minimum withdrawal amount is $2, you can withdraw your funds at any time. 5% tax charge applies to any withdrawal amount.

Is cloud mining safe?

Cloud mining can be a safe and secure way to mine cryptocurrencies, as long as you choose a reputable provider with a track record of success. At Anchor Cloud Mining, we prioritize the security and privacy of our customers, and we use advanced security measures to protect your data and your assets.

What's the minimum amount required to purchase hashrates?

There isn't a set minimum expense; you can buy hashrates with any sum, and the corresponding hash power will be delivered to your dashboard. Users also have the option to mine for free, but their earnings are limited due to the absence of contributing hashrates that accelerate the mining rate.

How do i increase my mining rate ?

You can increase your mining rate by buying more hashrates. The Increment happens authomatically, the new hashrate will be added to the existing hash rates to increase yield balance when mining.

How do I sign up for Anchor Cloud Mining?

Signing up is easy! Simply visit our website, create an account (REGISTER), buy your preferred hashrate. You can start mining and earning rewards immediately after registration. NOTE: Users have to visit the website daily to activate the mining mechanism.

Do I need any technical expertise to start Mining?

No, our platform is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise. We provide detailed instructions and support to help you get started.

Yes, we provide detailed statistics and reports on your mining activity and earnings, so you can monitor your progress and make informed decisions about your mining strategy.

Yes, only a 5% tax payment which is dynamic and can be adjusted at any time upon withdrawals, we are transparent about our fees and charges, and we do not impose any hidden fees or charges there are no additional costs or fees.

What is the tax for ?

It is a compulsory contribution to state revenue, levied by the government on our company for services and transactions being carried out by the company. Income received from mining is taxed as ordinary income based on the fair value of coins on the day they are received. Like any investment, cryptocurrency mining involves risks, and the market can be volatile. However, we strive to minimize risk by using advanced mining hardwares and softwares and by diversifying our mining activities across multiple cryptocurrency exchanges.
