ABOUT US

NEO IS AN OPEN SOURCE, COMMUNITY DRIVEN PLATFORM.

Neo is a software network that seeks to serve as a platform on top of which anyone can transact and create decentralized products and services. Neo is sometimes referred to as the “Ethereum of China” due to it sharing many similar features with its more popular counterpart, such as the ability to write decentralized applications (dapps) for decentralized exchanges, prediction markets and social networks, among others.

Further, the Neo network also offers a host of other features to its users, including a decentralized file storage system, an identity system, and an oracle system for feeding external information to it.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is cloud mining?

Cloud mining is the process of using remote data centers to mine cryptocurrencies. Instead of purchasing and maintaining your own mining hardware, you can rent computing power from a cloud mining provider and receive a share of the rewards based on the amount of computing power you've contributed.

How does Neo Cloud Mining work?

Neo Cloud Mining offers a user-friendly platform that allows you to purchase mining hashrates and start earning rewards immediately. Our data centers are located in secure facilities with high-speed internet connections, and we use the latest mining hardware and software to optimize your mining experience.

What cryptocurrencies can I mine in Neo Mining ?

We only offer hashrates for mining Neo Protocol. Our hashrates are designed to be flexible and affordable, allowing you to choose the option that best suits your needs and budget.

How much can I earn with Neo Cloud Mining?

Your earnings will depend on a variety of factors, including the hashrate you're using for mining, the amount of computing power you've contributed, and the current market conditions. We provide detailed information about expected earnings for each of our mining plans, so you can make an informed decision.

Cloud mining can be a safe and secure way to mine cryptocurrencies, as long as you choose a reputable provider with a track record of success. At Neo Cloud Mining, we prioritize the security and privacy of our customers, and we use advanced security measures to protect your data and your assets.
